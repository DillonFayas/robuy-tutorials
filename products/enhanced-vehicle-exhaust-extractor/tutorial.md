## FS Chassis

This system was built around A-Chassis, but is still compatible with FS Chassis if modifications are made. To adapt your truck, in the rig, go to Driving > Driverseat > Driving Controller, and delete line 91. After doing so, as long as the truck is put in park prior to attachment of the extractor, everything will function normally.
